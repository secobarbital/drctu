Organic, not organic?... Meat vs. Vegetarian?...Fat, non-fat? Sugar-in or Sugar-Free?Allergies?...Dairy? Gluten? Whey? Soy? It almost seems like there is no such thing as simple eating anymore.


Our fast-paced lives alone, do not allow us to always eat “sensibly.” Compounded with over processing of foods, there is no real way to get complete nourishment from our diet alone.  This is one of the main reasons why it is important to supplement.


How do you know what supplements to take? The most definitive way to find out what your body needs is to do a series of specific blood tests, and over time, determine what your body may be deficient in.  For most, this may be a difficult and trying task.  Generally, if you have a high quality product, your body will utilize what it needs, and discard the rest. 


I am my patient’s guinny-pig.  I have tried multiple supplements and weight loss products.  I don’t recommend anything that I haven’t tried myself.


Since our bodies are all different, results may vary.  There is no “magic” supplementation or weight loss diet, but there are high quality products that can  help diminish the guessing game.  Weight loss is often complicated by other health factors and things such as food sensitivity.  Inflammation in the body caused by food sensitivity can mask itself as weight gain. 


  Your ongoing health takes commitment, patience and diligence.
