  1. 1.Have your work station ergonomically evaluated.

  2. 2.Choose and use a supportive chair. I recommend both the Liberty chair and Saddle chair made by Human Scale.  Build a chair at www.humanscale.com  Bring in your specs and we’ll order your chair with a significant discount.

  3. 3. Utilize the 90 degree rule: 90 degree bend at the legs, with your feet flat on the floor. 90 degree bend at the arms and make sure your hands are freely moving over your keyboard.

  4. 4. Keep your books and monitor directly in front of you at eye level.  A book prop or reading stand will save your neck and tired shoulders! This is great for anyone who reads alot!!

  5. 5.Use a lightweight headset rather than cradling the phone with your neck and shoulder.
