Stretching not only allows muscles to function more optimally, but it helps to protect our joints. A stretched out muscle has more "driving power" than an unstretched muscle. Stretching also helps to "wake up" muscles so that they are better prepared to react under any unexpected movement/terrain.


HOW? Take a quick five minute walk to warm up the body. Do gentle held stretches prior to activity/exercise, and after.
